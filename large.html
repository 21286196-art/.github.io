Large Sized Dog Breeds

Large dog breeds possess strong physique and stable mentality. Despite their big appearance, most of them are gentle towards family members. They require larger living space and long daily walks, many varieties are trained as rescue or guard working dogs.

SCROLL DOWN ↓

German Shepherd

Smart, obedient and versatile, German Shepherds widely work as police, search and rescue dogs, also trustworthy family protectors.

Golden Retriever

With mild personality and golden silky fur, golden retrievers are patient with children and frequently used as guide dogs for disabled people.

Alaskan Malamute

Strong, loyal, and friendly, Alaskan Malamutes are built for cold climates and love being part of a family. They are gentle giants with a playful, affectionate side.
