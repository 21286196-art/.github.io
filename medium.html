Medium Sized Dog Breeds

Medium dogs own well-proportioned bodies with moderate exercise demands. Balanced in energy and character, these breeds fit nearly all family types. They can adapt to courtyard or apartment life and excel at accompanying kids for daily outdoor activities.

Shiba Inu

Originated from Japan, Shiba Inus have independent temperaments and neat red fur. Clever and clean, they are among the most trendy medium companion dogs.

Border Collie

Famous for top intelligence, Border Collies were originally sheep herding dogs. They need plenty of outdoor exercise and mental training.

Siberian Husky

Energetic and independent, huskies are known for their striking blue eyes and wolf-like appearance. They are playful, loyal, and love outdoor adventures.
