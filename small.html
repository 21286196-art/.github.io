Small Sized Dog Breeds

Small breed dogs feature compact body size and lightweight build, which makes them ideal for apartment raising. They usually have lively personalities, rely heavily on human company and consume less food and activity space.

SCROLL DOWN ↓

Pomeranian

Pomeranians have fluffy double coats and alert fox-like faces. They are outgoing, noisy and full of curiosity, popular indoor lap dogs.

Chihuahua

As one of the tiniest dog breeds, Chihuahuas are brave beyond their size, loyal to their single owner and easy to carry around.

Yorkshire Terrier

These tiny, silky-coated terriers are bold, confident, and surprisingly energetic. They thrive on attention and make lively, affectionate lap dogs.
